BETWEEN

The Origin Group Company (hereinafter referred to as "The Company")

AND

Origin.rw Platform User (hereinafter referred to as "The User")

This Agreement governs the relationship between The Company and The User regarding the submission, evaluation, sponsorship, and commercialization of business ideas, prototypes, or operating businesses (collectively referred to as "Business Projects").

1. Definitions

  • Origin.rw Platform: The digital interface provided by The ORIGIN GROUP Company for idea submission and project management.
  • Business Project: Any business idea, prototype, or operating business submitted by The Users.
  • Sponsorship: Financial or resource-based support provided by The ORIGIN GROUP Company or third-party sponsors.
  • Commission: The Company's share of revenue from the sale or licensing of a Business Project.
  • Confidential Information: Any non-public business, technical, or financial information disclosed during the Project evaluation process.

2. Modification of Agreement

  1. The Company reserves the exclusive right to modify, amend, or update this Agreement at any time without prior consultation with The User.
  2. Changes will be communicated via:
    • Email to the registered User address.
    • Platform notifications or dashboard announcements.
    • Publicly accessible version history log on the Platform.
  3. Notice Period:
    • Minor changes (e.g., typographical corrections, clarifications) take effect immediately upon posting.
    • Substantial changes (e.g., revenue share adjustments, IP rights modifications) require 15 days' notice before enforcement.
  4. User Options Upon Modification:
    • Continued use of the Platform after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.
    • If The User disagrees with substantial changes, they may:
      • Terminate this Agreement within the notice period which may or may not be followed by the termination the account held on origin.rw. If the user terminate, they will be subject to the compensation that may arise from the ORIGIN GROUP expenses- this include but not limited to Intellectual property generation cost, lawyer services costs, return of the sponsorship, investment given and the service offered to the user's business project. This will be calculated together with the user when the need to terminate the contract arises.
      • Request removal of their Business Projects from commercialization pipelines.
  5. Archived versions of this Agreement will be accessible for at least 2 years for dispute resolution purposes.

3. Submission & Ownership

  1. The User retains copyright ownership of submitted Projects but grants The Company:
    • A perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to evaluate, modify, and commercialize the Business Project.
    • The right to make derivative works (up to 20% modification of original content).
  2. Warranties: The User guarantees that:
    • They are the sole originator of the Project or have obtained all necessary rights.
    • The Project does not infringe any third-party IP rights.
  3. Company Discretion: The Company may reject Projects deemed:
    • Unoriginal.
    • Technically unfeasible.
    • Ethically or legally problematic.

4. Intellectual Property (IP) & Sponsorship

  1. IP Protection Process:
    • The Company will assess patent/trademark/copyright eligibility.
    • The User must cooperate in filing applications (costs borne as agreed separately).
  2. Sponsorship Terms:
    • Sponsorship does not imply equity transfer unless specified in a separate contract.
    • Sponsored Projects may be subject to additional milestones and reporting requirements.
  3. Patent filings may require:
    • Special category data (biometrics for inventors)
    • Additional consent forms for EU applicants

5. Revenue Sharing & Commercialization

  1. Commission Structure:
    • 40% of gross revenue to The Company.
    • 60% to The User (minus taxes and direct expenses).
  2. Payment Terms:
    • Quarterly payments with detailed accounting statements.
    • Right to audit upon reasonable request.
  3. Pricing Control: The Company determines all commercialization terms including:
    • Licensing fees.
    • Equity stakes in spin-off entities.
    • Royalty structures.
  4. Payment data processing:
    • Payment processors act as GDPR-compliant sub-processors
    • Audit rights for EU users regarding revenue calculations

6. Meetings & Negotiations

  1. Strict Communication Protocol:
    • All discussions with potential buyers/sponsors/investors require:
      • Prior written approval from The Company.
      • Participation of a Company representative.
  2. Breach Consequences: Unauthorized negotiations may result in:
    • Immediate termination.
    • Forfeiture of pending payments.
    • Legal action for damages.

8. Termination & Disputes

  1. Termination Clauses:
    • By User: 30 days written notice (commercialization rights persist for ongoing deals).
    • By Company: Immediate for breach; otherwise, 15 days' notice.
  2. Post-Termination:
    • The Company retains rights to Projects already in commercialization pipelines.
    • User may request project removal from inactive pipelines.
    • EU user data archived separately for regulatory compliance
    • Right to full erasure after mandatory retention periods
  3. Dispute Resolution:
    • Mandatory mediation before litigation.
    • Governing law: If the dispute arises are not resolved amicably in the 30 days, the disputes will be referred to the Jurisdiction in the court of business in the Government of Rwanda.
    • Each party bears its own legal costs unless gross misconduct is proven.

9. General Provisions

  • Force Majeure: Excused performance for events beyond reasonable control.
  • Assignment: The Company may transfer this Agreement; Users may not without written consent.
  • Severability: If any clause is invalid, remainder stays enforceable.
