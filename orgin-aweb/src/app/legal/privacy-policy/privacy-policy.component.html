1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register or submit Projects, we collect:

  • Full name, contact details (email, phone, address)
  • Educational/professional background
  • Government-issued ID (for IP filings)
  • Payment/banking details (for revenue sharing)

1.2 Project Information

  • All submitted materials (documents, designs, code, etc.)
  • Communication with our evaluation team
  • Feedback from experts/sponsors

1.3 Technical Data

  • IP addresses, device information
  • Usage patterns via cookies and analytics tools
  • Platform interaction logs

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Recipients

  • Experts/Evaluators: Under NDA for Project assessment
  • Sponsors/Investors: Anonymized data initially, then detailed under LOI
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law
  • Service Provider: Hosting, payment processors, etc.

3.2 International Transfers

Data may be transferred globally with safeguards:

  • EU/Swiss data: Standard Contractual Clauses
  • Other regions: Adequacy decisions or binding corporate rules
  • Data may be transferred outside EU/EEA to:
    • U.S.-based sponsors (under Standard Contractual Clauses)
    • UK processors (under Adequacy Decision)

4. Data Security

4.1 Protection Measures

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit
  • Annual penetration testing
  • Role-based access controls
  • Secure development lifecycle (SDL) practices

4.2 Breach Protocol

  • Notification within 72 hours of discovery
  • Remediation plan implementation
  • Regulatory reporting where required

10. GDPR-Specific Information

10.1 Lawful Basis & Consent (Article 6 GDPR)

By submitting a Project, you explicitly consent to:

  • Processing of personal data for evaluation/commercialization (Legal Basis: Contractual Necessity)
  • Transfers to third-party experts/sponsors (Legal Basis: Legitimate Interest)
  • Marketing communications (Opt-in checkbox required, withdrawable anytime)

10.2 Enhanced User Rights (Articles 15-22)

Right to Access & Portability

Upon verified request, we will provide:

  • Machine-readable copy of your personal data within 30 days
  • Detailed explanation of processing purposes

Right to Erasure

You may request deletion of:

  • Unused Project submissions (if no commercialization initiated)
  • Personal profile data (unless required for tax/legal compliance)

10.3 Data Subject Access Request (DSAR) Procedure:

  • Submit via: [Online Form] / [Email Template]
  • Response within 30 calendar days (extendable for complexity)
  • No fees unless manifestly unfounded

10.4 Breach Notification (Article 33)

In case of data breach:

  • 72-hour notification to supervisory authority
  • High-risk breaches communicated to users via email
  • Public disclosure on platform if widespread impact

10.5 Automated Decision-Making (Article 22)

Project evaluations involving AI:

  • Human review available upon request
  • Right to contest algorithmic assessments
  • Transparency reports on scoring criteria
